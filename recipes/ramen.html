Ponyo's Ramen

Description

Inspired by the heartwarming scenes from the beloved animated film "Ponyo," this ramen recipe captures the essence of comfort and simplicity.

Ponyo's Ramen is a delightful fusion of traditional Japanese flavors and the magical touch of Studio Ghibli's storytelling.

With a rich, savory broth, tender noodles, and fresh toppings, this ramen is perfect for fans of the movie and food lovers alike.

Ingredients

  • Broth :A flavorful base made with chicken or vegetable stock, soy sauce, mirin, and a hint of miso for depth.
  • Noodles : Fresh ramen noodles that provide the perfect chewy texture.
  • Protein : Slices of tender pork belly or a soft-boiled egg for added richness.
  • Toppings :Fresh vegetables such as spinach, green onions, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds to enhance the taste and presentation.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Broth :Simmer chicken or vegetable stock with soy sauce, mirin, and miso until well combined and aromatic.
  2. Cook the Noodles :Boil fresh ramen noodles until just tender, then drain and rinse under cold water.
  3. Prepare the Broth :Arrange the cooked noodles in a bowl, pour the hot broth over them, and add slices of pork belly or a soft-boiled egg.
  4. Add Toppings :Garnish with fresh spinach, chopped green onions, and sesame seeds.
