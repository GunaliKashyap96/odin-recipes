Howl's Moving Castle Calcifer's Bacon and Eggs

This hearty breakfast features the fiery demon Calcifer from "Howl's Moving Castle," represented by sizzling bacon and sunny-side-up egg

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Eggs
  • Toast or baguette slices

Instructions

  1. Cook the Bacon: Fry the bacon until crispy.
  2. Cook the Eggs: Fry the eggs sunny-side-up in the bacon fat, ensuring the yolks are runny.
  3. Assemble: Serve the eggs and bacon on a plate with toast or baguette slices on the side.
