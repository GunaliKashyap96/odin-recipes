Arrietty's Cream Stew Recipe

Description

Inspired by the cozy and comforting scenes from Studio Ghibli's "The Secret World of Arrietty," this cream stew is a heartwarming dish that captures the essence of homely goodness. Packed with tender vegetables, succulent chicken, and a rich, creamy broth, Arrietty's Cream Stew is perfect for a nourishing meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken and Broth
  • 1 lb (450g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 cups (950ml) chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • Vegetable
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 medium carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 cup (150g) frozen peas
  • 1 cup (150g) corn kernels

  • Cream Sauce
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups (475ml) milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream

  • Seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken and Broth:
    • In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a simmer.
    • Add the bay leaf and chicken pieces. Cook until the chicken is tender and fully cooked, about 10-15 minutes. Remove the chicken and set aside, keeping the broth in the pot.
  2. Cook the Vegetables:
    • Add the potatoes, carrots, and onion to the pot with the chicken broth. Simmer until the vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes.
  3. Make the Cream Sauce:
    • In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour and cook, stirring constantly, for about 1-2 minutes to make a roux.
    • Gradually whisk in the milk, ensuring there are no lumps. Continue to cook, stirring frequently, until the sauce thickens, about 5-7 minutes.
    • Stir in the heavy cream and season with salt, pepper, thyme, and parsley.
  4. Combine and Finish:
    • Add the cooked chicken, peas, and corn to the pot with the vegetables. Pour in the cream sauce and stir to combine.
    • Simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  5. Serve:
    • Ladle the creamy stew into bowls and serve hot. Enjoy the comforting and rich flavors reminiscent of Arrietty's small yet enchanting world.
